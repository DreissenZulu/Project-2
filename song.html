Babushka Boi

by A$AP Rocky

Hip Hop

2018

“BABUSHKA BOI” is supposedly a track off of Flacko’s next upcoming album, which was released on 28 August, 2019. The track was first previewed during his 2019 Injured Generation tour. The song’s title refers to a babushka, which is a headscarf tied under the chin, typical of those traditionally worn by Russian old women.
